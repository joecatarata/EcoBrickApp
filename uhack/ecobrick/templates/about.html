What is an Ecobrick?


An Ecobrick is a bottle packed solid with used plastic to make a reusable building block. Best of all, you don’t need any fancy machines, special skills, engineers or politicians to get started. All you need is a plastic bottle and a stick to start.

Why Ecobrick?


Ecobricks are cradle-to-cradle building blocks that can make all sorts of awesome stuff. Around the world people are using ecobricks to build for themselves and their communities. From indoor furniture, to gardens and parks, to structures– your imagination is the only limit!

Some other things to take note of...

*Use dry and cleaned up bottles and plastics.
*Pack your plastics in your bottles tightly using a stick!
*Use plastics only, don't include metal, glass and biodegradeables.
*Weigh your ecobrick before depositing to be sure!
